## üå∏ Logo

The Evermore logo features a delicate floral emblem that represents memory, life cycles, and care. It balances strength and softness ‚Äî the design is stylized, not overly intricate, making it feel modern yet meaningful.

**Logo Variants:**
- **Primary Logo:** Full logo with emblem and wordmark
- **Horizontal Logo:** Compact horizontal arrangement (used in navbar)
- **Wordmark:** Text-only version for tight spaces
- **Emblem:** Standalone emblem for icons and favicons
- **Simple Emblem:** Simplified version for small sizes

**Color Versions:**
- Golden Ochre (primary on light backgrounds)
- Midnight Moss (on parchment/light backgrounds)
- Charred Plum (alternative dark version)

---

## Usage Notes

- Pair serif headers with simple sans-serif body text for contrast.
- Maintain ample whitespace around the logo and content for a calm, respectful visual rhythm.
- Avoid overly bright or saturated tones ‚Äî subtlety reinforces the brand's emotional integrity.
